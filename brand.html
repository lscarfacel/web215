About Us

Lets talk a little bit about us

Thank you for checking us out!

We believe the more involve you are when learning a programming language the faster your will learn it. Like any other speaking languages. We are a local group that like to share, talk, and help others learn what we know.

What We Do

We help individuals interested in learning how to code succeed in their journey of learning. People who are curious about programming and students that are already learning a programming language.

How We Do It

We provide events, answers, one on one classes, and resources to enhance the learning of the future programmer. Our events offer interactivity in a casual setting so everyone feels confortable and is more engage in the conversation. Our resources and forums are available when a challenge becomes hard to solve.

The Way we see things

There is always room to inprove!

This is a local group composed of curoius individuals, students, proffesionals, and educators that want to share or learn programming. By getting together and becomming a big community, we will improve and learn different skills in the programming field. We will help each other get through the challenges of becoming a programmer. This will help our community to develop new skills and figure out a career path, grow our network, make new friends, and have fun while coding.
